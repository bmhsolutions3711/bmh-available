1. Introduction

BMH Solutions LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates a hotshot trucking business and uses automated SMS messaging technology to support internal business operations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information in connection with our SMS messaging system powered by Twilio.

By receiving SMS messages from us, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone numbers used to receive operational SMS notifications
  • Business contact information shared in the course of our trucking operations

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Message delivery status and timestamps
  • Carrier and device type information (provided by Twilio)
  • Location data embedded in Google Maps links within SMS messages

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for the following internal business purposes:

  • Sending automated SMS notifications to company personnel regarding load pickups and deliveries
  • Providing Google Maps navigation links for pickup and delivery addresses
  • Internal operational coordination between drivers and business management
  • Improving our automated notification systems

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose unrelated to our direct business operations.

4. SMS Messaging Disclosures

Our SMS messaging system is used exclusively for internal business operational notifications:

  • Messages are sent to authorized company personnel only
  • Message content includes load details, pickup/delivery addresses, and Google Maps navigation links
  • Message frequency varies based on business activity (typically 1–5 messages per load processed)
  • Message and data rates may apply based on your carrier plan
  • To opt out, reply STOP to any message
  • For help, reply HELP or contact us at bmhsolutions3711@gmail.com

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share only with:

  • Twilio Inc. — our SMS provider (twilio.com/legal/privacy)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

6. Data Retention

We retain SMS-related data only as long as necessary for business operations and legal compliance. You may request deletion by contacting us directly.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Our SMS system uses Twilio's secure infrastructure with industry-standard encryption.

8. Your Rights

  • Opt out at any time by replying STOP
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information
